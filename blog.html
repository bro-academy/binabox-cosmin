The Basilisks NFT

Gaming

20 Jun 2022

Basilisks aren't just creatures - they're digital apex predators. Forged on the Ethereum chain and raised in a metaverse colosseum, they live to dominate, duel, and devour. Every one of them is an NFT with unique DNA, traits, and bloodlust.

  • In thi game, your Basilisk is the warrior. You breed it. You name it. You train it. And when you're ready, you send it into the arena - not to play, but to survive.
  • Each NFT Basilisk has a set of traits, mutations, and skills, minted immutably on-chain. The arena? A full 3D metaverse battleground with wagers, livestreams, and faction wars. Players don't just watch - they stake tokens, call hits, and climb leaderboards.
  • The game's AI learns from every move. If your Basilisk gets too strong - it becomes a target. If it wins? You earn crypto, glory, and power.
  • Unique DNA structures
  • PvP-enabled arenas
  • Permanent death mode
  • Metaverse integration
